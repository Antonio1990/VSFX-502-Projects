SHADER DOCUMENTATION NOT FOUND

Either the RenderMan software you are using does not have information about its standard shaders or the entries in the prefs.ini that "point" Cutter to the HTML documents is not setup correcly.

Example Paths

PRMAN 11.0.1 

path.pixar.docs              "C:/Program Files/Pixar/docs-5.5/reference/shaders"
path.pixar.docs.shaders.toc  "tocShaders.html"
path.pixar.docs.shaders.ext  ".3s.html"

BMRT

path.bmrt.docs.shaders      "C:/BMRT2.6/doc/html"
path.bmrt.docs.shaders.toc  "index.html"
path.bmrt.docs.shaders.ext  ".html"

AIR

path.air.docs.shaders      "C:/air/doc/shaders"
path.air.docs.shaders.toc  "shaders.html"
path.air.docs.shaders.ext  ".html"

3DELIGHT

path.3delight.docs.shaders      "C:/3delight/doc/html"
path.3delight.docs.shaders.toc  "3delight.html"
path.3delight.docs.shaders.ext  ".html"

BMRT and 3Delight do not have web pages that describe specific shaders. However, they do provide the source code for their shaders so by looking at a shaders source code you may be able to figure out what the individual parameters are controlling.

PRMAN and especially AIR have extensive documentation on their "built-in" shaders.
